Search Engine Optimisation

10 ways to improve your websites SEO

  1. Keyword Optimization - Every page on your website should follow a specific keyword strategy in order to make an impact on Googles search engine algorithms, with each page having its own specific keyword.
    (Whitman, 2018)
  2. Title Tags - Search engine algorithms analyse headlines of every webpage which makes them very important to increasing your SEO effectiveness. Each title should have a keyword near the beginning, allowing Google to see the relevance of the content. Along with this, headlines should be compelling, in order to spark interest and actr a s a call to action.
    (Whitman, 2018)
  3. Meta Description - These are always displayed below the title in a Google search result so it is one of the most 2 most important things a user sees before deciding to visit the site or not.
    (Whitman, 2018)
  4. Publish High-Quality Content - No matter what SEO tricks you can pull off, you wont get traffic without actually providing high quality content as well, by making your webpage more enticing, it also increases the effectiveness of the SEO.
    (Whitman, 2018)
  5. SEO Audit - The website needs to be constantly maintained and cleaned. Establishing weak points and imporving them definitely helps increase traffic to the site and therefore increases SEO effectiveness.
    (Whitman, 2018)
  6. Image tags - Because people have images all over thier websites it is important to make sure these all have appropriate tags. It helps Google to understand what the image is and then effectively recoomend the site to the right searches.
    (Mitchell, 2015)
  7. Backlinks - THese are incoming hyperlinks from one webpage to another. It is important these are substantial and relevant to the site as a whole. By having others link to your site you increase the webpages chances of being displayed in a search.
    (Mitchell, 2015)
  8. Make your page speed faster - Speed is something not often thought about in terms of SEO, but it is very important. People dont want to be waiting 10-15 seconds for a page to load, and neither does Google. by speeding up the page, Google is more likely to display the page because people are going to be happier loading into a fast webpage.
    (Patel, 2019)
  9. Link to relevant content - Much like backlinking this is the other side of it, by linking others websites you are significantly increasing the effectiveness of your webpages SEO, by linking to other webpages you encourage others to link to you, and soon enough you will have a small hub of connected webpages.
    (Patel, 2019)
  10. Anchor Text - While on the topic of links, the anchor text in a hyperlink must be relevant to the page, else the webpages ranking in SEO wil go down. For example "Click here" and "Read more" are bad examples, and it is much better to describe the page or topic being linked to.
    (Moore, 2017)
